Instructions

1: Keep your laptop screen straight.

2: To test, move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear on the screen.

3: Press the play button.

4: Move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

NOTE: The red paddle is yours, which will move according to the movement of your right wrist.
